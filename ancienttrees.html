From the collection

A few of the trees waiting for you

Amsterdam, Netherlands
The Westerkerk Elm
Approx. 280 years old

Anne Frank wrote about this tree from her hiding place. It stood outside the Secret Annex until 2010, when a storm finally brought it down — but not before cuttings were distributed to eight countries.

Lisbon, Portugal
Jacaranda do Jardim da Estrela
Approx. 180 years old

A jacaranda of unusual scale in one of Lisbon's oldest public gardens. In May, its violet canopy turns the surrounding paths into something closer to a painting than a park.

Palermo, Italy
Il Ficus Secolare
Approx. 150 years old

A Moreton Bay fig with a canopy that covers over a thousand square metres. Its aerial roots have formed secondary trunks over the decades, making it feel less like a single tree and more like a forest.
